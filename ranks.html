Wanderer #1

Description: As a Wanderer, you've just stepped into this vast world, eager to explore its wonders and mysteries. You're a newcomer, taking your first steps on an epic adventure, uncertain of what lies ahead but full of curiosity and excitement. Every corner you turn and every hill you climb brings new discoveries and experiences, shaping you into the hero you're destined to become. 

Requirements: Requires 10 hours of playtime, 1000 blocks broken, 500 blocks placed, 5 Exp Levels, 5000 blocks traveled by foot, and 10 votes. 

Artisan #2

Description: he Artisan is a master of creation, skilled in the art of crafting. Your hands deftly mold raw materials into beautiful and functional objects, from simple tools to intricate works of art. You take pride in your craftsmanship, knowing that your creations are not just items but a reflection of your skill and dedication. Others admire your work and seek out your creations, knowing that they are of the highest quality. 

Requirements: Requires 50 hours of playtime, 10,000 blocks broken, 5,000 blocks placed, 25 Exp Levels, 25,000 blocks traveled by foot, and 25 votes.

Trader #3

Description: The Trader is a savvy entrepreneur, diving into the bustling economy of the realm. You understand the value of goods and services, and you excel at buying, selling, and trading to turn a profit. Whether you're wheeling and dealing in the marketplace or striking deals with fellow adventurers, you know how to make the most of every opportunity. Your keen eye for business and your silver tongue make you a force to be reckoned with in the world of commerce. 

Requirements: Requires 100 hours of playtime, 50,000 blocks broken, 25,000 blocks placed, 50 Exp Levels, 50,000 blocks traveled by foot, and 50 votes.
